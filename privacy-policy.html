Privacy Policy

Last updated: 12/22/25

Welcome to coexist. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the coexist mobile application.

coexist is designed to help adults find compatible roommates and shared living situations.

coexist is intended only for users aged 18 and older. By using the Service, you confirm that you are at least 18 years old. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18.

Information We Collect

We collect information you voluntarily provide and limited usage data to operate and improve the app.

Personal Information You Provide

  • Name or display name
  • Email address
  • Account profile details
  • City, state, or general area you identify yourself as being "from"
  • Preferences or answers related to roommate compatibility
  • Messages or content you choose to share within the app
  • Analytics
Important: coexist does not collect precise location data (GPS), real-time location, or background location tracking.

Usage & Technical Information

  • App activity and interactions
  • Device type, operating system, and app version
  • Log data such as IP address and timestamps

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Match users for roommate compatibility
  • Display relevant profile information to other users
  • Improve app functionality and user experience
  • Communicate important updates or support messages
  • Maintain safety, security, and platform integrity

Location Information

coexist does not collect or track your precise location.

Any location-related information shown in the app (such as city or state) is:

  • Manually entered by you
  • Used only for matching and display purposes
  • Editable or removable by you through your profile settings

How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information.

We may share information only:

  • With trusted service providers (such as authentication, database hosting, or analytics tools like Firebase)
  • When required by law or legal process
  • To protect the rights, safety, or security of users or the platform
  • In connection with a business transaction (such as a merger or acquisition)

User-Generated Content & Visibility

Information you choose to include in your profile or messages may be visible to other coexist users. You are responsible for the information you choose to share.

Data Storage & Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your information. While we strive to protect your data, no system can be guaranteed to be 100% secure.

Your Rights & Choices

You may:

  • Access or update your account information
  • Edit or remove profile details (including location text)
  • Delete your account at any time

To request assistance, contact us at [your support email].

Third-Party Services

coexist uses third-party services (such as Firebase) to support authentication, data storage, and app functionality. These services operate under their own privacy policies.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date and may be communicated within the app.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, contact us at:
